# CareAxis Cloud

Working healthcare marketing website with route-driven pages, About Us and Privacy Policy routes, public demo/contact lead capture, and a dismissible cookie notice.

Public lead forms post to `https://dev.indemnisezmoi.fr/api/lead` and show:

`Details saved. We will reach you shortly.`

Auth, signup, reset, and portal forms are presentation flows only and are not wired to the lead API.

## Required Routes

- `/about-us`
- `/privacy-policy`

Keep the website production-facing. Do not describe public forms, cookie notices, or lead capture as fake, prototype-only, or local-only.
